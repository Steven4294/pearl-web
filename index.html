About Pearl

Networked Biometric Identity is Inevitable

The frictionless authentication experience enabled by biometric technology is limited to personal devices, leaving the clunkiness of authentication unsolved. However, strong consumer, enterprise, and government demand for networked identity has sparred notable investment in biometric infrastructure. Ultimately, incentive neutral networks will harmonize authentication across applications while giving users full control of their identity.
